Carrot Tarator

What is Carrot Tarator

This yogurt salad recipe is something that you will want on every dinner table. Feel free to substitute zucchini in place of carrots if you prefer. Or why not have the best of both worlds and mix the two?

Ingredients

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 3 walnuts
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 tbsp yogurt
  • salt

How to Make Tarator Step-By-Step

  1. Grate the carrots and garlic cloves, coarsely chop the walnuts.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan. Add the grated carrots and garlic and saut√© for 5 minutes until the carrots soften. Let it cool.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the yogurt with salt.
  4. Add the cooked and cooled carrots and chopped walnuts and mix well.
  5. Transfer to a serving plate and drizzle with olive oil.
